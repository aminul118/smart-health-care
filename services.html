How we works

Our Key Services

At Smart Healthcare and Research Ltd., we offer a comprehensive range of services designed to revolutionize healthcare for women and children. Our approach combines advanced technology, evidence-based practices, and a deep commitment to patient care. Explore our core services below:

Online Consultation Platform

BAN-GDM is Our flagship service, the online consultation platform provides secure and private access to healthcare professionals from the comfort of your home. This user-friendly platform ensures that women and families can receive online expert medical advice, especially for diabetes in pregnancy cases, consultations, and follow-up care without the need for physical visits, breaking down barriers to healthcare access.

Features

  • Secure, private consultations
  • Accessible anywhere in a fixed time
  • Focused on Maternal and Child Health

Research and Analysis

Our experienced research team is at the forefront of maternal and child health research. The expert team assists in conducting research in public health, clinical trials, analysis of secondary data and so on. We ensure that the latest evidence and the highest quality of research protocols back all of our research works. Our research efforts also contribute to the national healthcare community, fostering knowledge and innovation.

Focus Areas:

  • Maternal and Child Health Research
  • Public Health Research
  • Clinical trials and health outcomes research

Training Programs for Healthcare Professionals

We believe that well-trained healthcare professionals are the cornerstone of excellent patient care. Our specialized training programs focus on enhancing the skills and knowledge of healthcare providers in maternal and child health, equipping them with the latest techniques and insights to offer exceptional care.

Training Includes:

  • Workshops and seminars
  • Hands-on clinical training
  • Digital healthcare management skills

Patient empowerment

Empowering women and parents with the knowledge to make informed healthcare decisions is a core part of our mission. We provide a wide range of educational materials and resources to help individuals understand maternal and child health, nutrition, and preventive care, leading to healthier outcomes for families.

Resources Provided:

  • Guideline on maternal and child healthcare and nutrition
  • Skills for self-management in GDM
  • Interactive educational tools
  • Health tips and expert advice

Community Outreach

Our commitment to healthcare extends beyond individual consultations. We are actively engaged in outreach programs designed to bring quality healthcare services to underserved and marginalized populations. By collaborating with govt., NGOs and community organizations, we aim to ensure access and care to every woman and child

Outreach Programs:

  • Healthcare access for underserved and hard-to-reach populations
  • Health education workshops and clinics
  • Support for local health initiatives

Research Publications and Academic Collaboration

As leaders in healthcare innovation, we regularly publish our research findings in esteemed academic journals. By collaborating with educational institutions and participating in global research initiatives, we contribute to the advancement of maternal and child healthcare on a global scale.

Academic Contributions:

  • Publish research findings on maternal and child health
  • Collaboration with academic institutions
  • Participation in global healthcare conferences

At Smart Healthcare and Research Ltd., each of our services is designed with the goal of improving healthcare access, quality, and outcomes for women and children. We are committed to continuous innovation and compassionate care, ensuring that our clients receive the best possible support on their healthcare journey.
